Privacy Policy - StudyTrack
1. Information Collection and Use
The StudyTrack app was built as a personal, local utility tool. We do not collect, store on external servers, or share any personal information or usage data from our users.

2. Local Storage
All data generated within the application (such as subjects, dates, and study durations) is saved exclusively locally on your device using an internal database (SQLite/Room). None of this information is transmitted over the internet.

3. App Permissions
StudyTrack does not request or require any sensitive permissions (such as access to the camera, contacts, or location) to function, as its sole purpose is the manual logging of study routines.

4. Third-Party Services
The app currently does not use any third-party services for tracking (Analytics) or displaying advertisements.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We advise you to review this page periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at: cafezeiro.dev@gmail.com.
